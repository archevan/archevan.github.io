Strategy Statement

We decided that our “dream team” would consist of people with very different skill sets. We decided that this would be the most prudent course of action because we believe that a balanced team will be the most effective when dealing with a revolution. We chose many people from varying disciplines. We chose enlightenment thinkers, battle-hardened military leaders, and even ruthless radicals. Instead of approaching the challenge of creating a team in a centralized and specific way, we utilized our resources in moderation. We wholeheartedly believe that this is the best course of action.

Rating System

We rated our potential candidates based on four attributes, which were Military Experience, Popularity, Political Savvy, and Intellect. We used a graphic that resembles a progress bar. There a nine total blocks in the progress bar, there are three red block, three yellow blocks, and three green blocks. Each individual category had its own graphic. Each person received a score from one through nine in each category. With this information we were able to determine the strengths of each revolutionary. We used that information to create an order of selection. Political Savvy and Intellect were weighed more heavily which meant that they were evaluated first. Military Experience and Popularity were evaluated after the initial evaluation. This process was repeated for each of the 31 dossiers.
